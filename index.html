About the label

We amplify
silence.

Mute Division is an independent label built for artists who exist outside the mainstream. We work at the intersection of experimental electronic, ambient, and underground club music.

Our mission is simple: give visionary artists the infrastructure they deserve, without compromising their sound or creative control.
